(dated validate / disconfirm events), this keeps the **current standings** — each item under its present grade,
so anyone can read the whole field's evidential state at a glance.

**Updated in the SAME commit that changes any grade.** An item moves between sections here exactly when its
grade moves; a disconfirmed hypothesis moves to *Disconfirmed* here and gets its dated line in `MEASUREMENTS.md`.
