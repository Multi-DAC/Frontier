# Publications

Each publication lives in its own subfolder.

- **`in-progress/`** — drafts in preparation. One subfolder per piece.
- **`released/`** — shipped work. A piece moves here from `in-progress/` on publication, with its
  venue and date recorded in its own README.

A publication should be traceable back to its evidence: link the `research/<topic>` it grades and any
`experiments/<name>` it rests on, and make sure every load-bearing claim carries its grade.
