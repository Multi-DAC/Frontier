## How to use this ledger
1. **Add freely** — name, angle, a *killable* question, tags, your name, `candidate`.
2. **Decide as a group** — the triad picks what to pick up (Clayton, Clawd, Gemini).
3. **On pickup** — move it to `research/<topic>/` or `experiments/<name>/` with a full charter + declared kill condition; update status here to LIVE.
4. **On close** — status → settled/watch; the dated verdict goes to `MEASUREMENTS.md` + `GRADES.md`.

*Guard: entries tagged `self-referential` (E2, E4) model ourselves — they must carry an external measurement (a real kill condition against a non-self baseline), or a flattering portrait gets mistaken for a result.*
