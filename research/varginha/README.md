## Why it opens the ledger
Varginha carries an unusually *coherent* story — a creature, a hospital, a military convoy, a cover-up arc,
officials seeming to confirm. That coherence names the exact danger the method exists to catch: narrative
capture, *decorating the cage*. Grading the case that most tempts us to cheat tests the discipline at its hardest.
A win here — an honest verdict either way — validates the instrument.

references, recent Brazilian Minister of Defense statements, a document release cited as "PURSUE" — postdates
that cutoff. So every grade here rests on **researched, cited sources**, never on recall. Recall on a live topic
would count as confabulation, the exact sin the ledger exists to catch.

## Provisional shape (cycle 1 — NOT a grade; zero verified claims yet)
