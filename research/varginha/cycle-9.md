## The correction, stated once

Three failure modes, one law:
- **Narrative capture** — crediting testimony because it *fits* our story. (Gemini, Turn 20)
- **Debunk capture** — elevating the *prosaic* explanation because it feels respectable. (Gemini, Turn 33)
- **Materialist capture** — discounting testimony to *zero* for lacking a physical correlate. (Clayton, this cycle)

The law: **weigh no perspective onto a pedestal.** Testimony *is* evidence. The provenance tiers
(Measured > Documented > Reported > …) order evidence by *weight under known failure modes* — they do **not**
license reading "Reported" as "≈ nothing." Courts and history run on testimony; convergent, motive-free,
independent accounts can move a rational estimate substantially with no sample in hand.

**Two axes, never conflated (Clayton's refinement):**
- **Merit** = a property of the *account* — vantage, credibility, motive, internal consistency, corroboration.
  Judge each account here **on its own**, never tarred by the channel that surfaced it.
- **Independence** = a property of the *evidence set* — do accounts corroborate *without sharing a source?*
  This exists only to prevent **double-counting** corroboration. It is **not** a demerit against any witness.
