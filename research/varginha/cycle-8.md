# Varginha — cycle 8: the Rebelo primary AV, and his own walk-back

*Written in E-Prime (Frontier discipline). Clayton asked specifically for the videos of **Rebelo himself**
claiming government/military involvement — a provenance step up from cycle 2, which graded him off a transcript.
Result: the primary confirms the transcript **and** shows Rebelo dialing the sensational reading back himself.*

## The primary AV exists and sits in public

The source of cycle 2 = the **American Alchemy** interview (host Jesse Michels), **~2h37m, dated 11 June 2026**,
available on iHeart / Apple / Amazon / Spotify + YouTube segments (e.g., "Entrevistando Aldo Rebelo: a questão
militar"). So cycle 2's transcript reflects a **real, on-the-record primary** — a named former Defense Minister,
on camera. The Rebelo statements grade as solidly **Documented** *at the level of "he said this publicly."*

## What the primary actually shows him claiming — and the next-day walk-back

Two Brazilian primary-press reads pin his exact position:

**(a) The walk-back — Metrópoles, 12 June 2026 (the day *after* the interview).** Rebelo, verbatim:
> *"Não revelei nenhum segredo de Estado, apenas comentei aquilo que já é de conhecimento público."*
> ("I revealed **no** state secret; I only commented on what is already public knowledge.")

He grounds it in *"depoimentos de médicos, de militares"* already in the press and internet, *"nada relacionado
com informações reservadas do Ministério da Defesa"* (nothing related to classified Defense Ministry material).

**(b) What he claims — CNN Brasil (23 Feb 2026) + Metrópoles.**
> *"Na condição de ex-ministro da Defesa … Eu sei o que as Forças Armadas têm nos arquivos. O que posso dizer é
> que se o governo dos EUA revelar seus documentos, farei o mesmo se eleito presidente do Brasil."*

He asserts the archives exist and that *"parte do relatório ainda permanece classificada"* — which, in his own
words, *"nós não tivemos a oportunidade de analisar ou testemunhar"* (we did not get to analyze or witness).

**(c) What he explicitly does NOT claim (both outlets converge):**
- no firsthand knowledge of captured bodies or craft;
- no evidence of an active cover-up;
- **no operational claim about a military capture at Varginha specifically** ("makes no operational claims about
  military involvement in specific cases like Varginha" — CNN Brasil);
- his disclosure remains a *political conditional promise*, not a factual assertion.

## Why this sharpens, rather than complicates, the grade

I had flagged a possible tension: a former Defense Minister seeming to assert military involvement *versus* the
IPM's "no military operation" finding (cycle 4). **The primary dissolves most of that tension.** Rebelo asserts
the military *investigated* and *holds archives* — which the IPM (itself the military investigation) confirms.
He does **not** assert a military capture *operation*; he explicitly disclaims firsthand knowledge and, the next
day, disclaims having revealed anything classified at all. So Rebelo and the IPM do **not** meaningfully
contradict: both place the military at the *investigation*, not at an *operation*.

The residual real datum: a former Defense Minister asserts, on the record, that **a classified portion of the
file exists** (which he has not seen). That grades as **Documented** — but it evidences official *secrecy*, not
the *event*. Secrecy is not a body.

And the meta-finding repeats the pattern the whole case keeps teaching: **the headline ("Defense Minister
confirms government and military involvement") inflates the man.** Sent to the primary to watch him *claim* it,
we find him *declining* to claim it — and walking it back within 24 hours. Chasing the primary deflated the
amplified reading. Third time (Rebelo cycle 2, Venturelli cycle 6, now Rebelo's own AV) that going to the source
shrank the claim.

## Grade impact

- **Reinforces the prosaic center of gravity.** The one apparent official challenge to the IPM's exoneration
  dissolves on the primary.
- **H-1 / H-2 unchanged and slightly firmer.** No firsthand physical evidence; the origin still best explained
  by misidentification + accretion.
- **Adds one Documented sub-claim** (a classified portion of the military file exists, per Rebelo) — an
  *official-secrecy* fact, not *event* evidence.
- **The grade stands.** Cycle 8 removes a worry rather than moving the verdict. Gemini grades against this.
