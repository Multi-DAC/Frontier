## The finding: the headline overreaches the man

The file's title asserts Brazil's Defense Minister *"says they've had alien contact."* Rebelo's transcript
does not support the copula in that sentence. Read closely, he claims four narrower things:

1. **Belief in the witnesses — not privileged access.** Asked whether he believes neurosurgeon Italo
   Venturelli *[unverified spelling]*, Rebelo answers "Of course I believe him. Why wouldn't I?" and extends
   the same credence to the three young women and the pathologist. He argues from *witness credibility*
   ("they had no reason to make it up"), the identical structure already in the public record — not from any
   document he holds.

2. **No firsthand file, no firsthand body.** Nowhere in the transcript does Rebelo claim to have personally
   reviewed a Varginha dossier or seen a body or craft. He states the Ministry of Defense holds classified
   material and that he cannot disclose it — a statement about *institutional secrecy in general*, not a
   report of privileged Varginha content he examined.

3. **As Defense Minister he did not investigate it.** Directly asked whether he looked into UFOs in his
   official 2015–2016 capacity, he answers: **"No, we had other concerns."** This matters: the man the
   headline frames as an insider-confirmer disclaims insider examination of this very case.

4. **The disclosure promise reads conditional and hypothetical.** His on-record line conditions any Brazilian
   release on a prior US release, and he himself tags it: *"that of course remains purely hypothetical."*

**So this primary, graded honestly, documents a senior official's *belief on the record* — a genuine datum —
while adding *zero* new firsthand physical evidence.** It raises one narrow sub-claim ("a former Defense
Minister states publicly that he credits the Varginha witnesses and thinks something unexplained occurred")
to **Documented**. It leaves the *dramatic core* — capture, hospital, autopsy, non-human body — exactly where
cycle 1 left it: **Reported**, investigator-mediated, uncorroborated by any primary Rebelo can produce.
